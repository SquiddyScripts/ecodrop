- **Hunt et al. 2022** — Lift Energy Storage Technology (LEST) → elevator integration direction
- **Hunt et al. 2023** — Underground Gravity Energy Storage
- **Elsayed et al. 2022** — Taguchi optimization of gravity storage
- **Luo et al. 2015** — EES technology overview (benchmark efficiencies)
- **Kermani et al. 2021 / Zhang et al. 2019 / Dalala et al.** — elevator regenerative drives & DC microgrids
