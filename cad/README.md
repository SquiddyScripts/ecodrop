## `third-party/` — Community models used in visualizations

These were **not modeled by the team**. They are GrabCAD community downloads used as visual
props in the 2026 interactive elevator demos (cab, counterweight frame, hoistway hardware).
See [third-party/SOURCES.md](third-party/SOURCES.md) — they are included for reproducibility
of the demos, with authorship credited to their original creators.
