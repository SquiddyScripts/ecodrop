Baseline: 50 kg mass, 3 m drop, 1471.5 J gravitational PE input. Mechanical losses (rope, bearing, gearbox, motor) are nearly identical across all four systems. System-specific losses account for the entire performance difference between systems.
Fig 3
Energy input to charge vs. electrical energy recovered on discharge per cycle
Energy in to charge (PE input, 1471.5 J — identical for all systems) Energy out on discharge (electrical)
All systems receive identical gravitational PE input (1471.5 J). The gap between each gray and colored bar represents total cycle losses. The ratio of output to input equals round-trip efficiency.
